The miner is used as a proof of work web miner on our website, you mine other coins(you don't need to care what) and get rewarded in WorkTokens(WTK) or WorkTHR for every succesfully accepted share.

Live miner on https://cfcbazar.42web.io/miner/

Uses our coinimp public key.

Which ever coins are mined does not matter, they are converted to BNB to fuel the pool for our WorkTHR/WTK on pancakeswap and the value of the WorkToken on the BNB chain. You can trade the WorkTHR/WTK on the pancakeswap exchange and other exchanges.
